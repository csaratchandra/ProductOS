## Category Statement

ProductOS is a governed AI-native operating system for product managers.

## Ideal Customer

The best early fit is a PM or PM-led product team that already has evidence, planning, and delivery work happening across too many disconnected tools and wants a stronger operating system for decisions, communication, and follow-through.

## Acute Problem

PMs do not usually fail because they cannot generate more text.

They fail because product truth drifts across artifacts, docs, updates, and stakeholder conversations faster than anyone can keep aligned manually.

## Why Existing Tools Are Not Enough

Most tools solve one narrow slice:

- project tracking
- note capture
- roadmap presentation
- competitive intelligence
- AI drafting

ProductOS is aimed at the coordination gap between those slices.

## Differentiation

ProductOS differentiates on five points:

- source-of-truth artifacts instead of freeform-only AI output
- readable docs generated from the same operating state
- governed presentation and communication paths
- bounded AI persona roles with validation lanes
- Ralph-loop improvement discipline for high-stakes slices

## Proof Points

- the current repo already uses ProductOS as its self-hosting workspace
- V6.0.0 establishes lifecycle traceability through `release_readiness` while preserving governed readable-doc, presentation, and market-intelligence surfaces
- the benchmark model defines explicit PM outcome targets rather than vague AI productivity claims

## Claim Boundaries

Do not position ProductOS as:

- a PM replacement
- a fully autonomous product-management agent
- an unattended safe market-monitoring system

The stronger truthful claim is that ProductOS gives PMs a governed operating system and AI leverage without hiding review, evidence, or approval boundaries.
