Status: ready

Owner: ProductOS PM

## Problem Statement

Product managers on B2B product teams lose high-value time because recurring status and coordination outputs must be manually reconstructed across fragmented sources.

## Who Is Affected

- Product managers running recurring stakeholder communication
- Leaders who need timely, consistent updates
- Cross-functional teams waiting on clarified decisions, risks, and next steps

## Why This Matters Now

AI-assisted drafting raises the bar for trusted operating systems. The bottleneck is no longer only writing; it is maintaining reliable state, evidence, and communication quality.

## Evidence And Confidence

- leadership review evidence flagged recurring status reconstruction pain
- prototype validation showed useful drafts when source state was structured and current
- benchmark evidence shows materially lower time to identify blocked routes and retrieve relevant context

## Current Workaround Cost

- repeated manual PM synthesis
- inconsistent stakeholder updates
- higher risk of stale or unsupported communication

## Constraints

- outputs must stay evidence-backed
- provenance must remain visible
- runtime and readable documents must remain synchronized

## Recommendation

Continue using this problem framing as the anchor for the roadmap and PRD, and extend ProductOS so the same problem can be communicated through standard readable documents.
