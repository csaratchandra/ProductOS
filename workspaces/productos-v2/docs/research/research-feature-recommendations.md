# Research And Auto-Research Feature Recommendations

Status: reviewed  
Owner: ProductOS PM  
Audience: PM Builder, PM Operator, PM Leader / Communicator  
Updated at: 2026-03-21  
Source artifact ids: `research_feature_recommendation_brief_ws_productos_v2_v4_1`

## Recommended Features

### 1. Competitor watchlists and deltas

Give PMs persistent watchlists with delta-first summaries, not one-off market scans.

### 2. Guided deep-research plans

Require ProductOS to propose a plan, show source scope, and preserve citations before producing a report.

### 3. Mixed-source grounding

Blend public market evidence, ProductOS memory, and ask-driven inputs while keeping source tiers visible.

### 4. Retrieval-first context bridge

Expose approved context to agents before expanding to wider write access.

### 5. Ralph governance loop

Make inspect, review, validate, implement, fix, and revalidate a visible loop for every high-stakes research slice.

## Prioritization

Watchlists, guided deep research, Ralph governance, unattended-refresh proof, and the internal leadership-digest path now form the current governed baseline.

The next bounded expansion should focus on external publication adapters and broader packaging rather than re-opening the already-proved refresh and internal-distribution path.
