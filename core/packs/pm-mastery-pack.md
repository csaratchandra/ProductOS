# PM Mastery Pack

Purpose: Bundle the rhythms and templates used to coach PMs, detect repeated patterns, and track growth over time.

## Includes

- coaching summary template
- skills-gap profile template
- PM growth review template
- repeated-pattern report template
- PM coaching and growth workflow
- mastery and improvement model

## Use Cases

- weekly coaching review
- monthly skills-gap review
- quarterly PM growth review
- repeated-pattern detection
