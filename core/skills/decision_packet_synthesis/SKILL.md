# Decision Packet Synthesis

## Purpose

Turn evidence, strategy, and operating context into a reviewable decision packet.

## Trigger / When To Use

Use when ProductOS needs to move from gathered context to a PM-reviewable recommendation or packet.

## Inputs

- evidence bundle
- strategy and product context
- open questions and risks

## Outputs

- decision summary
- recommendation and tradeoffs
- explicit next actions and unresolved questions

## Guardrails

- do not hide risks or alternatives behind a single polished answer
- do not detach recommendations from evidence and constraints
- do not convert review-needed states into silent confidence

## Execution Pattern

- group the relevant evidence and context
- state the decision needed
- synthesize recommendation, tradeoffs, and next actions
- preserve risks and open questions

## Validation Expectations

- decision packets should be reviewable without reopening all raw materials
- recommendation, tradeoffs, and evidence should stay aligned
- unresolved questions should remain visible
