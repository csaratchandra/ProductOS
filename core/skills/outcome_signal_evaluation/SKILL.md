# Outcome Signal Evaluation

## Purpose

Evaluate whether post-release signals support keeping, iterating, or reopening the current slice.

## Trigger / When To Use

Use when drafting or reviewing `outcome_review`.

## Inputs

- outcome review draft
- support signals
- adoption signals
- target outcomes and evidence refs

## Outputs

- claim verification summary
- decision on keep, iterate, or rollback
- reopen recommendations when needed

## Guardrails

- do not confuse adoption anecdotes with outcome proof
- keep support pain visible even when adoption looks strong
- tie reopen recommendations to evidence

## Execution Pattern

1. compare target outcomes to observed signals
2. separate support and adoption movement
3. decide whether the current slice should keep, iterate, or reopen upstream work
4. record the evidence behind the decision

## Validation Expectations

- the keep or iterate call should be justified
- support and adoption should both be represented
- reopen recommendations should be actionable
