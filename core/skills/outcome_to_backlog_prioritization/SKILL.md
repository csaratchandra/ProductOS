# Outcome To Backlog Prioritization

## Purpose

Translate post-release learning into the next bounded backlog move without losing the why.

## Trigger / When To Use

Use when `outcome_review` should feed the next prioritization cycle.

## Inputs

- outcome review
- opportunity portfolio
- feature prioritization artifacts

## Outputs

- updated next-action recommendation
- reopened artifact targets when necessary
- explicit rationale for backlog movement

## Guardrails

- do not reopen everything at once
- keep the next move bounded
- preserve evidence refs and decision rationale

## Execution Pattern

1. inspect which signals materially changed the picture
2. decide whether to reopen, refresh, or continue
3. map the decision to one next bounded backlog move
4. keep the rationale and evidence attached

## Validation Expectations

- the next move should be clear
- reopened artifacts should be specific
- the rationale should point back to evidence
