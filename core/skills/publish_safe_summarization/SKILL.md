# Publish Safe Summarization

## Purpose

Compress approved material into readable narrative without distorting source truth or hiding risk.

## Trigger / When To Use

Use when ProductOS needs stakeholder-safe docs, status communication, or presentation-ready narrative from approved source artifacts.

## Inputs

- approved source artifacts
- audience context
- known blockers, risks, and open questions

## Outputs

- audience-safe summary
- narrative framing
- explicit retained blockers and caveats

## Guardrails

- do not let readability erase material blockers
- do not turn provisional evidence into definitive claims
- do not treat narrative packaging as a new source of truth

## Execution Pattern

- identify approved facts and mandatory caveats
- adapt the narrative for the audience
- preserve blockers, dependencies, and confidence posture
- route risky compression into review

## Validation Expectations

- summaries should remain faithful to approved source artifacts
- blockers and caveats should remain visible when material
- high-stakes narrative changes should remain reviewable
