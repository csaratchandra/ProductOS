# Monthly Product Review Workflow

Purpose: Produce a monthly product review that connects workspace execution, outcomes, risks, and leadership decisions in one coherent operating view.

## Inputs

- portfolio update
- portfolio state
- status updates
- major decisions and risks
- release and increment state

## Outputs

- monthly product review summary
- leadership-ready decision framing
- cross-product dependency and risk view

## Rules

- connect product execution to outcome and portfolio value
- make leadership decisions and tradeoffs explicit
- summarize shared dependencies and escalations across products
- distinguish confirmed performance from assumptions and unresolved risk
