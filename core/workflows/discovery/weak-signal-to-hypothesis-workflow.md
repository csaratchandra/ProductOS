# Weak Signal To Hypothesis Workflow

Purpose: Convert weak, noisy, or fragmented market, customer, stakeholder, and delivery signals into a structured `signal_hypothesis_brief` before the PM commits to full discovery.

## Inputs

- `change_event`
- customer pulse and research inputs
- support, stakeholder, and market signals
- optional prior `strategic_memory_record`

## Outputs

- `signal_hypothesis_brief`
- optional `handoff_contract` to research, prototype-validation, or strategy review

## Companion templates

- `../../templates/discovery/signal_hypothesis_brief.md`
- `../../templates/research/research_notebook.md`
- `../../templates/research/research_brief.md`

## Rules

- require at least two independent signals before labeling a pattern as emerging
- distinguish noise, watch items, emerging patterns, and decision-relevant signals explicitly
- record what would need to become true before the pattern should change roadmap or investment logic
- preserve traceability to the raw signals so PM judgment can inspect the basis
- do not convert weak signals directly into commitments
