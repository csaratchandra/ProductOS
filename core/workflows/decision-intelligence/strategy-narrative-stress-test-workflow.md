# Strategy Narrative Stress Test Workflow

Purpose: Pressure-test a strategy, recommendation, or roadmap narrative before commitment by challenging it from multiple stakeholder and business lenses.

## Inputs

- `strategy_option_set`
- `stakeholder_incentive_map`
- `opportunity_advantage_brief`
- optional `decision_premortem`

## Outputs

- stress-test summary
- fragility notes for the decision queue
- recommendation to commit, revise, or gather more evidence

## Rules

- challenge the narrative from finance, sales, engineering, customer, and competitor perspectives
- surface objection patterns, not just isolated criticisms
- distinguish fixable narrative weakness from underlying strategic weakness
- preserve the strongest objection and the best available rebuttal for each lens
