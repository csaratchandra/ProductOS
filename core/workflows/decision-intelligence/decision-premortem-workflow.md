# Decision Premortem Workflow

Purpose: Run a structured pre-commitment failure analysis for major bets so ProductOS can surface reversal triggers and early warning indicators before investment.

## Inputs

- `strategy_option_set`
- `portfolio_state`
- `stakeholder_incentive_map`
- supporting research and decision artifacts

## Outputs

- `decision_premortem`
- recommendation to proceed, revise, or defer

## Rules

- identify likely failure causes before commitment, not after launch
- separate failure causes from early warning indicators and reversal triggers
- define what success would have to look like within the decision horizon
- attach premortem outputs to the related decision record for later outcome review
