# Agentic Market Intelligence Workflow

Purpose: Produce a governed market-intelligence pack that compares direct PM competitors, indirect workflow products, agentic research software, and CI tools, then converts the findings into ProductOS research and auto-research recommendations.

## Inputs

- PM market-intelligence question
- current `research_notebook`, `competitor_dossier`, `market_analysis_brief`, or `landscape_matrix`
- official product pages and help docs
- secondary research, reviews, or analyst coverage when needed
- existing ProductOS benchmark and validation context

## Outputs

- refreshed `research_notebook`
- capability-level `landscape_matrix`
- `competitor_dossier`
- `market_analysis_brief`
- `research_feature_recommendation_brief`

## Rules

- cover direct PM tools, indirect workflow products, agentic research tools, and CI platforms when the question is category-shaping
- use official sources for baseline capability claims and preserve checked dates
- use secondary evidence to validate blind spots, adoption reality, or contradictions
- keep capability extraction comparative instead of writing isolated vendor summaries
- separate ProductOS opportunities into `research`, `auto_research`, and `validation_governance`
- route the finished slice into the Ralph loop before release promotion or broad adoption claims
