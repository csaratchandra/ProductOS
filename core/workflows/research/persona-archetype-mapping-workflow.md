# Persona Archetype Mapping Workflow

Purpose: Produce a `persona_archetype_pack` that captures buyer, user, champion, blocker, operator, executive, or influencer archetypes explicitly.

## Inputs

- PM persona question
- target segment context
- source note cards, interviews, reviews, or prior research

## Outputs

- `persona_archetype_pack`

## Rules

- distinguish persona identity from archetype role
- preserve goals, pains, triggers, blockers, and buying influence separately
- allow the same persona to appear in more than one archetype mode where needed
- every archetype should carry evidence references
