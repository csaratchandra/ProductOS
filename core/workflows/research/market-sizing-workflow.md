# Market Sizing Workflow

Purpose: Produce a `market_sizing_brief` with explicit assumptions, formula path, and confidence notes.

## Inputs

- PM market question
- source note cards, reports, or market inputs

## Outputs

- `market_sizing_brief`

## Rules

- show TAM, SAM, and SOM separately
- make the sizing method explicit as top-down, bottom-up, or blended
- make every major assumption traceable to a source or an explicit estimate
- include sensitivity notes where the estimate is especially assumption-heavy
- do not present speculative math as high-confidence fact
