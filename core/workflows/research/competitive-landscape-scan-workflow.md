# Competitive Landscape Scan Workflow

Purpose: Build a fast but decision-useful competitive view from URLs, notes, reviews, and prior research.

## Inputs

- competitor URLs or names
- optional `source_note_card`
- optional prior `research_notebook`

## Outputs

- `landscape_matrix`
- `competitor_dossier`

## Rules

- compare at least three competitors or explicitly record why the market is sparse
- resolve named competitors for the actual ICP and workflow lane before treating the scan as decision-ready
- classify competitors as direct, indirect, tech platform, vendor, or startup where evidence supports it
- capture positioning, target customer, pricing signal, go-to-market motion, strengths, weaknesses, implications, evidence refs, confidence, and checked dates
- decompose competitors comparatively by workflow coverage, implementation posture, proof model, and expansion path when that affects the wedge
- keep the matrix comparative rather than turning it into three isolated summaries
- preserve evidence references for every important implication
- grade the output as `draft`, `reviewable`, or `decision_ready` instead of letting weak scans silently drive posture
