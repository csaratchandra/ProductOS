# Segmentation Mapping Workflow

Purpose: Produce a `segment_map` that makes the segmentation lens explicit instead of collapsing all customers into one generic segment view.

## Inputs

- PM segmentation question
- source note cards, feedback, market inputs, or prior research

## Outputs

- `segment_map`

## Rules

- choose and record the segmentation lens explicitly
- allow multiple valid segmentations for the same market when lenses differ
- preserve evidence references for segment definitions and prioritization
- distinguish attractiveness from urgency
