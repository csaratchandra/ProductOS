# Market Analysis Workflow

Purpose: Produce a `market_analysis_brief` covering category dynamics, trends, dependencies, and risks beyond sizing math.

## Inputs

- PM market analysis question
- market notes, competitor scans, analyst reports, and prior research

## Outputs

- `market_analysis_brief`

## Rules

- do not collapse market analysis into TAM/SAM/SOM math alone
- capture category shifts, ecosystem dependencies, and moat pressure explicitly
- keep key uncertainties visible instead of forcing certainty
- preserve source artifacts for major conclusions
