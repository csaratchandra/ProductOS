# Research Notebook To Synthesis Workflow

Purpose: Turn a `research_notebook` into a decision-ready `research_brief`.

## Inputs

- `research_notebook`
- linked `source_note_card`
- optional `consultant_report_digest`, `landscape_matrix`, or `market_sizing_brief`

## Outputs

- `research_brief`

## Rules

- use note cards as the evidence base rather than raw source files
- keep strategic implications, contradictions, and synthesis provenance explicit
- recommend more research when the notebook is rich in notes but weak in support for a decision
