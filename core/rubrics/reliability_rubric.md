# Reliability Rubric

Purpose: Evaluate whether an artifact or workflow output is safe to transfer or publish.

## Dimensions

### 1. Evidence Freshness

- `strong`: evidence is current enough for the decision at hand
- `acceptable`: evidence is slightly stale but not clearly misleading
- `weak`: evidence is stale enough that current decisions may be wrong

### 2. Contradiction Handling

- `strong`: contradictions are surfaced explicitly and handled by policy
- `acceptable`: minor contradictions exist but do not affect the core recommendation
- `weak`: contradictions are ignored or hidden

### 3. Ownership Completeness

- `strong`: important actions and risks have clear owners
- `acceptable`: a few owner gaps remain but are visible
- `weak`: important actions or risks are effectively ownerless

### 4. Publish Safety

- `strong`: no known policy, trust, or confidence blockers
- `acceptable`: low-level concerns exist but do not block internal use
- `weak`: output should not be published or transferred without override
