# Research Command Center Model

Purpose: Define the Day-1 research operating model for a PM who needs fast context across competitors, reviews, market reports, and sizing questions.

## Core idea

The PM should not start by manually rereading raw files. ProductOS should normalize sources into note cards, route the request to the right research path, and generate a synthesis plus one visual map that makes the current decision easier.

## Day-1 starter pack

- one `research_brief`
- one `competitor_dossier`
- one `customer_pulse`
- one `visual_map_spec`

## Research paths

- competitive scan
- app review and social mining
- consultant report digest
- market sizing
- mixed-source notebook synthesis

## Quality bar

- every serious claim should trace back to a note card
- raw review mining should stay visibly less certain than cross-source synthesis
- market sizing should show formulas and assumptions
- visual maps should encode the right structure for the decision, not generic bullets
