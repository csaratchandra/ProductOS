# ProductOS Presentation Narrative Quality Checks

Purpose: Define the quality bar for V4 presentation narratives before they are considered publishable.

## Required Checks

### 1. Decision Clarity

- the primary ask is visible on the first meaningful slide
- the audience should not need presenter translation to know the recommendation

### 2. Evidence Placement

- every material claim shows source-linked evidence or an explicit appendix trace
- counterevidence and uncertainty are not hidden

### 3. Audience Fit

- density matches the target audience
- terminology matches the audience's familiarity level
- the mode is appropriate for live, async, memo, or customer-safe delivery

### 4. Narrative Discipline

- the story arc advances logically from answer to evidence to risk to next action
- decorative transitions do not replace actual reasoning

### 5. Governance Visibility

- blockers, confidence gaps, and fidelity limits remain visible
- the deck does not imply approval that the underlying artifacts do not have

## Minimum Publish Bar

- answer-first opening for decision decks
- explicit risk treatment when the decision is material
- source visibility for all decision-driving slides
- publish-check pass for the target format
- manual validation for Tier 3 communication
